Chocolate Chip Cookies

Sweet and Savory Cookies

These classic chocolate chip cookies are crispy on the edges and chewy in the center, offering the perfect balance of textures. Made with a blend of granulated and brown sugar, these cookies have a rich, caramel-like flavor that complements the semisweet chocolate chips. Perfect for any occasion, these cookies are sure to melt in your mouth and leave you wanting more!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp saltk
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded spoonfuls of batter onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to completely cool.
  9. Serve and Enjoy!
