Chicken Tikka Masala


Flavorful Indian Classic

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular indian dish featuring marinated chicken, cooked in a rich, creamy tomato sauce. The blend of spices, including cumin, coriander, and garam masala, combines to create a flavor that is distinct and delicious. This dish is usually paired with rice and naan bread, and is a must try for anyone who loves bold, aromatic cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground tumeric
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, tumeric, paprika, garam masala, cayenne pepper, and salt. Add the chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour, overnight preferred.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is browned and cooked through. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the onion, garlic, and ginger. Cook until the onion is soft and translucent.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in the cream and return the chicken to the skillet. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice and naan.
